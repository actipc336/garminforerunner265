**Key Features of the Garmin Forerunner 265**
-------------------------------------------
The Garmin Forerunner 265 is packed with features that cater to both fitness tracking and smartwatch functionalities. Below is an in-depth look at the standout features of the device:

- **Garmin Elevate Heart Rate Monitor:** The Forerunner 265 is equipped with Garmin's proprietary Elevate optical heart rate sensor, which continuously monitors your heart rate throughout the day and during workouts. This feature helps you stay within your target heart rate zone, ensuring that you maximize the effectiveness of your training sessions.

- **Advanced Sleep Tracking:** Garmin's sleep tracking feature provides valuable insights into your sleep patterns, allowing you to monitor the quality and duration of your rest. Understanding your sleep stages—such as light, deep, and REM sleep—can help you optimize your recovery and improve your overall health.

- **Pulse Ox Sensor:** The Forerunner 265 includes a Pulse Ox sensor that measures your blood oxygen saturation levels, which can be especially useful for high-altitude training and monitoring overall health. This sensor helps provide a comprehensive view of your wellness, enabling you to make informed decisions about your fitness routines.

- **Garmin Coach Integration:** For runners looking to improve their performance, the Forerunner 265 is compatible with Garmin Coach, a feature that provides personalized training plans tailored to your fitness level and goals. Whether you're preparing for a 5K or a marathon, Garmin Coach will guide you with structured workouts and race day strategies.

- **Smart Notifications and Connectivity:** Stay connected with smart notifications for incoming calls, messages, and app alerts. You can also use the Garmin Connect app to sync your fitness data, track your progress, and connect with other users. This seamless integration ensures that your Garmin Forerunner 265 works as an extension of your digital life.

- **Water-Resistant Design:** The Forerunner 265 is designed to withstand water exposure, making it suitable for swimming and other water-based activities. With its water resistance rating, the device can be worn in the pool without concern for damage, allowing athletes to track their swim metrics and performance.
